# Romans

Infantry and Cavalry civilization 
-  Villagers gather, build, and repair +5% faster 
-  Infantry armor upgrade effects are doubled 
-  Scorpions cost -50% gold 
-  Galley-line and Dromons +1 melee/+1 pierce armor

**Unique Unit:**
Centurion (Cavalry), Legionary (Infantry)

**Unique Techs:**

-  Ballistas (Scorpions attack +33% faster; Galley-line +2 attack) 
-  Comitatenses (Militia-line, Knight-line, and Centurions train +50% faster and receive a charge attack)

**Team Bonus:**
Scorpions minimum range reduced

(highest unit the civ can reach on each line; units listed as missing are NOT available; lines not listed at all are entirely unavailable to this civ)

**Archery Range**

**Stable**

**Siege Workshop**
- Ram line: up to **Siege Ram**

- Scorpion line: up to **Heavy Scorpion**

**Dock**
- Galley line: up to **Galleon**
- Fire Ship line: up to **Fast Fire Ship**

**Monastery**
- Monk: up to **Monk**
