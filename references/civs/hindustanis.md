# Hindustanis

Camel and Gunpowder civilization 
-  Villagers cost -8/13/18/23% in Dark/Feudal/Castle/ Imperial Age 
-  Camel Riders attack +20% faster 
-  Gunpowder Units +1 melee/+1 pierce armor 
-  Can build Caravanserai in Imperial Age

**Unique Units:**
Ghulam (Infantry), Imperial Camel Rider (Cavalry)

**Unique Techs:**

-  Grand Trunk Road (All gold income +10% faster; Market trading fee reduced to 10%) 
-  Shatagni (Hand Cannoneers +2 range)

**Team Bonus:**
Scout Cavalry-line and Camel Units +2 attack vs. buildings
